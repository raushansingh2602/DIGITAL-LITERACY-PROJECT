# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students )

##  Prevention Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone — even if they claim to be from the bank.
- Always verify links before clicking, especially those received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or payment apps.
- Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
- Do not install apps from unknown sources; use only Google Play Store or official websites.
- **UPI Safety:** Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — PIN is only required for sending money.
- **UPI Safety:** Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept collect requests from unknown persons.
- Review bank and UPI transaction alerts regularly to detect suspicious activity early.
- Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public computers.


## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- National Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
- Inform your bank or payment app immediately to block further transactions.
