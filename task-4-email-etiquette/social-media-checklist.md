# Social Media Responsibility Checklist 

##  Do's
- Think before you post — make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- Maintain privacy — keep your personal information secure using privacy settings.
- Use social media for learning and networking (e.g., academic groups, professional platforms).
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Be respectful and polite in comments and online interactions.

##  Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details like your address, phone number, or passwords.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments.
- Avoid oversharing personal life details that could harm your reputation.
- Do not spread fake news or unverified information.
